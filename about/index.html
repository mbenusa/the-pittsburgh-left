The Pittsburgh Left is a blog.

These are the stories of me and my friends in Pittsburgh. We are twenty-somethings with our whole lives ahead of us. Some of us read books, others play board games, but most of all, we drink beer and liquor, sometimes wine when we want to be sad. This isn't a chronicle of Pittsburgh life, left politics, or culture reviews, but all of those will appear on this page. Life is about passions, and this is a passion project about the things I love.
